Using the mod:
--------------

This mod adds a diamond fist, that is much faster than the default fist in creative mode. Of cause you can use the diamond fist in survival too.

To craft one you need 7 diamonds and 2 steel ingots, placed following way:

diamond   diamond   diamond
 steel     steel    diamond
diamond   diamond   diamond


The diamond fist has endless uses.
